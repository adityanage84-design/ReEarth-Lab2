# ReEarth Local Knowledge Context

## Product Overview
ReEarth is a subscription-based EPR compliance and verified recycling platform.

Tagline:

> Where Recycling Meets Planet Restoration

Founder:

> Aditya Nage

Sector:

> EPR Compliance & Verified Recycling

Business model:

> B2B subscription model combining SaaS dashboard workflows with compliance services.

## Core Problem
Plastic-producing SMEs and D2C brands must comply with India's EPR regulations, but the current ecosystem is fragmented, manual, and low-trust.

Key customer pains:

1. Recycling costs are unpredictable.
2. Compliance is manual and paper-heavy.
3. Businesses cannot easily verify recycler claims.
4. Fake or unreliable certificates create audit risk.
5. SMEs may lose around 50 hours per month chasing paperwork and recyclers.
6. There is no simple real-time dashboard for EPR compliance status.

## ReEarth Solution
ReEarth connects businesses to a verified recycler network and provides:

1. CPCB-aligned certificate issuance.
2. Public certificate verification through ID/QR/hash.
3. Real-time compliance dashboard.
4. Dedicated compliance manager.
5. Audit-ready monthly and annual reports.
6. Predictable subscription pricing.

### Secondary
- Mid-size consumer brands without in-house compliance teams

## Main Persona
### Compliance-Constrained Founder
Role: Founder or operations lead at a plastic-packaging D2C brand.

Pain: Spends significant time chasing recyclers and assembling audit paperwork.

Fear: A fake certificate appears during a CPCB audit and causes penalties or brand damage.

Goal: Predictable cost, verified documentation, and one dashboard to check compliance status.

Trigger to buy: Audit deadline, CPCB compliance deadline, or ESG reporting requirement from investor/retail partner.

## Secondary Persona
### Overstretched Compliance Consultant
Role: Independent CA or compliance consultant serving multiple SME clients.

Pain: Tracks EPR status across many clients using spreadsheets and email.

Fear: A client gets penalized and blames the consultant.

Goal: A trusted platform to refer clients to, with status visibility per client.

## MVP Scope
In scope:

1. Recycler onboarding and verification workflow.
2. Customer onboarding and compliance mapping.
3. Certificate issuance and authentication engine.
4. Basic real-time compliance dashboard.
5. Subscription billing.
6. Compliance report exports.

Out of scope for MVP:

1. Physical waste collection logistics managed in-house.
2. International EPR frameworks.
3. Public marketplace for EPR credit trading.
4. Native mobile app.

## Certificate Verification Flow
1. Recycler logs processed waste volume against a subscriber allocation.
2. System checks volume against verified capacity and historical patterns.
3. If checks pass, a digital certificate is created.
4. Certificate receives unique ID, QR code, and hash.
5. Subscriber can download certificate from dashboard.
6. Third party can verify certificate through public verification page.

## Compliance Dashboard
The dashboard should show:

1. Obligation target.
2. Achieved recycling volume.
3. Compliance status: On Track, At Risk, or Overdue.
4. Certificate history.
5. Downloadable reports.
6. Monthly compliance review status.

For mid-size SMEs.

### Scale

Includes Growth features plus priority recycler allocation, audit-ready report packages, and regulatory alerting.
